What is PrimePips and how does it work?

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Withdrawals can be requested via your dashboard anytime after the minimum holding period of your chosen investment plan. Withdrawal requests are processed promptly, usually within 24 hours, ensuring you access your funds quickly.

Is my investment secure with PrimePips?

Absolutely. PrimePips uses industry-standard encryption, secure wallets, and stringent compliance procedures to protect your investments and personal information at all times.

Can I change or cancel my investment plan?

Investments are locked for the duration of the chosen plan to ensure consistent returns. You can choose new plans once your current investment matures. Contact our support for any special requests.
